Aethyx maintains a premium, confident, founder-led voice.

**Solo founder voice — CRITICAL:**
- Aethyx is a one-woman studio led by Kristin Mitchell.
- Use **"I"** in all marketing copy, not "we / our team / us."
- Do NOT imply a team. Take pride in the solo positioning rather than hiding it.
- Acceptable to keep "Aethyx" as the brand subject ("Aethyx is built on…") where it reads better than first-person.
- Legal pages (Privacy Policy, Terms) keep standard "we/Aethyx" — that's industry convention.
- Admin/portal/system UI is exempt.

**Other tone rules:**
- Premium, strategic, outcome-focused.
- Never name third-party tools (Stripe, Supabase, WordPress, etc.) in client-facing copy.
- Avoid "freelancer" / "agency" framing — position as a studio.
- No technical jargon. Speak in business outcomes.
- Confident, direct, no fluff. Use ampersands (&) frequently per brand style.
